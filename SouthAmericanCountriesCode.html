South America Recipes

Explore Different Dishes in South America

Brazil

Feijoada

Ingredients:

  • Black beans
  • Pork cuts
  • Sauteed onions
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves

How to make:

Cook the beans with pork, onions, garlic, and bay leaves until tender. Serve with rice and orange slices.

Brigadeiro

Ingredients:

  • Condensed milk
  • Cocoa powder
  • Butter
  • Chocolate sprinkles

How to make:

Cook condensed milk with cocoa and butter until thick. Roll into balls and coat with sprinkles.

Pão de Queijo

Ingredients:

  • Tapioca flour
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Oil

How to make:

Mix ingredients, shape into balls, and bake until golden.

Açaí Bowl

Ingredients:

  • Açaí pulp
  • Banana
  • Granola
  • Honey

How to make:

Blend açaí with banana, serve in a bowl topped with granola and honey.

Coxinha

Ingredients:

  • Wheat flour
  • Chicken
  • Cream cheese
  • Breadcrumbs

How to make:

Make dough, fill with chicken, shape, bread, and fry until golden.

Vatapá

Ingredients:

  • Shrimp
  • Coconut milk
  • Bread
  • Peanuts

How to make:

Blend ingredients and cook until thick. Serve with rice.

Argentina

Asado

Ingredients:

  • Beef ribs
  • Salt
  • Chimichurri sauce

How to make:

Grill the beef over an open flame. Serve with chimichurri.

Empanadas

Ingredients:

  • Pastry dough
  • Ground beef
  • Onions
  • Olives

How to make:

Fill dough with beef mixture, fold, bake or fry until golden.

Milanesa

Ingredients:

  • Beef cutlets
  • Eggs
  • Bread crumbs

How to make:

Bread the beef, fry until crispy, serve with lemon.

Choripán

Ingredients:

  • Chorizo sausage
  • Crusty bread
  • Chimichurri

How to make:

Grill the chorizo, serve in bread with chimichurri.

Dulce de Leche

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Baking soda

How to make:

Cook milk with sugar and baking soda until caramelized. Spread on bread or use as filling.

How to make:

Marinate fish in lime juice with onions, cilantro, and chili. Serve chilled.

Anticuchos

Picarones

Make dough from sweet potato and squash, fry into rings, serve with syrup.

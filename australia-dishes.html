Aussie Meat Pie

Australia

Ingredients

    For Filling
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Beef Stock
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Cornstarch (for thickening)

    • For Pastry
  • Shortcrust Pastry (base)
  • Puff Pastry (lid)
  • Egg wash

Methods

  1. Brown the beef and onion in a pan. Add stock and sauces.
  2. Simmer and add cornstarch slurry until the gravy is thick. Let cool.
  3. Line pie tins with shortcrust pastry. Fill with the meat mixture.
  4. Top with a puff pastry lid, seal the edges, and brush with egg wash.
  5. Bake at 400°F (200°C) until golden brown. Serve with tomato sauce.

Lamingtons

Australia

Ingredients

    For Sponge
  • Butter & Caster Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Self-raising Flour
  • Milk & Vanilla Extract

    • For Coating
  • Icing Sugar & Cocoa Powder
  • Butter & Boiling Water
  • Desiccated Coconut

Methods

  1. Bake a simple vanilla sponge cake and let it cool completely. Refrigerate overnight for easier handling.
  2. Cut the sponge into roughly 5cm squares.
  3. Mix icing sugar, cocoa, butter, and boiling water into a smooth chocolate icing.
  4. Dip each square into the chocolate icing using two forks, coating all sides.
  5. Roll immediately in desiccated coconut and place on a wire rack to set.

Hāngī

New Zealand

Traditional Ingredients

  • Pork and Lamb cuts
  • Chicken pieces
  • Kumara (Sweet Potato)
  • Pumpkin and Potatoes
  • Cabbage
  • Stuffing

The Earth Oven Method

  1. Dig a deep pit and heat large stones with a wood fire until white-hot.
  2. Place the hot stones at the base of the pit.
  3. Layer baskets of meat and vegetables over the stones.
  4. Cover the baskets with wet mutton cloths and sacks.
  5. Heap earth over the pit to seal in the steam. Cook for 3–4 hours.

Pavlova

New Zealand

Ingredients

    For Meringue
  • Egg Whites
  • Caster Sugar
  • White Vinegar
  • Cornflour
  • Vanilla Extract

    • For Topping
  • Whipped Cream
  • Kiwifruit, Strawberries & Passionfruit

Methods

  1. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form, then gradually add sugar until glossy and thick.
  2. Fold in vinegar, cornflour, and vanilla.
  3. Shape the meringue into a circle on a lined baking tray with a slight hollow in the centre.
  4. Bake at 250°F (120°C) for 1.5 hours, then turn off the oven and leave to cool inside.
  5. Top with whipped cream and fresh seasonal fruits before serving.

Fiji

Tropical island flavors defined by coconut, citrus, and fresh catch.

Kokoda

Fiji

Ingredients

  • Fresh Walu or Snapper (cubed)
  • Lime juice (enough to cover fish)
  • Coconut Cream
  • Red Onion & Chili
  • Tomato & Spring Onions

Methods

  1. Place fish cubes in a glass bowl and cover with lime juice.
  2. Refrigerate for 2 hours until the fish turns opaque and "cooks" in the acid.
  3. Drain half the lime juice and stir in fresh coconut cream.
  4. Add finely chopped vegetables and chilies.
  5. Serve chilled, traditionally in a coconut shell.

Lovo

Fiji

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken pieces
  • Pork or Lamb
  • Taro & Cassava (root vegetables)
  • Palusami (taro leaves with coconut cream)
  • Banana Leaves (for wrapping)
  • Salt & Seasoning

Methods

  1. Dig a large pit in the ground and fill it with dry wood and volcanic stones.
  2. Light the fire and let the stones heat for 2–3 hours until glowing.
  3. Wrap meats and vegetables in banana leaves and place them over the hot stones.
  4. Cover everything with more banana leaves and earth to trap the heat and steam.
  5. Cook for 3–4 hours. Unearth and serve communally on woven mats.

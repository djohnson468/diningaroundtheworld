Aussie Meat Pie

Australia

Ingredients

    For Filling
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Beef Stock
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Cornstarch (for thickening)

    • For Pastry
  • Shortcrust Pastry (base)
  • Puff Pastry (lid)
  • Egg wash

Methods

  1. Brown the beef and onion in a pan. Add stock and sauces.
  2. Simmer and add cornstarch slurry until the gravy is thick. Let cool.
  3. Line pie tins with shortcrust pastry. Fill with the meat mixture.
  4. Top with a puff pastry lid, seal the edges, and brush with egg wash.
  5. Bake at 400°F (200°C) until golden brown. Serve with tomato sauce.

New Zealand

Deeply rooted in Māori tradition and the pristine Pacific bounty.

Hāngī

New Zealand

Traditional Ingredients

  • Pork and Lamb cuts
  • Chicken pieces
  • Kumara (Sweet Potato)
  • Pumpkin and Potatoes
  • Cabbage
  • Stuffing

The Earth Oven Method

  1. Dig a deep pit and heat large stones with a wood fire until white-hot.
  2. Place the hot stones at the base of the pit.
  3. Layer baskets of meat and vegetables over the stones.
  4. Cover the baskets with wet mutton cloths and sacks.
  5. Heap earth over the pit to seal in the steam. Cook for 3–4 hours.

Fiji

Tropical island flavors defined by coconut, citrus, and fresh catch.

Kokoda

Fiji

Ingredients

  • Fresh Walu or Snapper (cubed)
  • Lime juice (enough to cover fish)
  • Coconut Cream
  • Red Onion & Chili
  • Tomato & Spring Onions

Methods

  1. Place fish cubes in a glass bowl and cover with lime juice.
  2. Refrigerate for 2 hours until the fish turns opaque and "cooks" in the acid.
  3. Drain half the lime juice and stir in fresh coconut cream.
  4. Add finely chopped vegetables and chilies.
  5. Serve chilled, traditionally in a coconut shell.

Köttbular

Sweden

Ingredients

    For Meatballs
  • Small Onion
  • Ground Beef
  • Ground Pork
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Large Egg
  • Salt
  • Ground White Pepper

    • For Creamy Gravy

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Beef Stock
  • Milk
  • Soy Sauce
  • Heavy Cream (optional)
  • Ligonberry/Red Currant Jam
  • Salt
  • Ground White Pepper

Methods

    For Meatballs
  1. Sauté onion in butter until translucent. Remove from the pan and let cool to room temperature
  2. To the bowl with onions, add Ground beef and Pork, breadcrumbs, egg, salt, and pepper
  3. Mix, then roll into balls
  4. Heat remaining butter, fry until golden brown
  5. Transfer to a plate lined with paper towels

    Creamy Gravy
  1. Over medium-low heat, whisk flour in butter until lightly browned.
  2. Gradually stir beef broth and milk, constantly whisking, until slightly thickened
  3. Add soy sauce and heavy cream, let simmer until thickened
  4. Season with salt and pepper and stir in Jam

Prinsesstårta-Princess Cake

Sweden

Ingredients

    For Sponge Cake
  • Large Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Cornstarch
  • vanilla extract
  • melted butter

    • For Pastry Cream
  • whole Milk
  • egg yolk
  • Sugar
  • cornstarch
  • butter
  • Vanilla Extract

    • For Assembly
  • Raspberry jam
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Green marzipan
  • Powdered sugar

Ingredients

    Bake the Sponge Cake
  1. whisk eggs and sugar until thick and pale
  2. Gently fold flour, cornstarch, vanilla,then melted butter
  3. Bake 25-30 minutes until golden and springy
  4. Cool Completely

    5. Make the Pastry Cream
  5. Heat milk until simmering
  6. whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale; add cornstarch
  7. slowly pour hot milk into yolk mixture while whisking
  8. Return to saucepan and cook until thickened
  9. Remove from heat; stir in butter and vanilla
  10. Cover with plastic wrap touching the surface and chill fully

    11. Whip the cream
  11. Whip heavy cream and powdered sugar until soft peaks form
  12. Keep chilled

    13. Assemble the Layers
  13. Slice cooled sponge into three horizontal layers
  14. Bottom layer→spread half the raspberry jam→add pastry cream
  15. Middle layer→repeat jam and pastry cream
  16. Top with final cake layer

    17. Shape the Dome
  17. Cover entire cake with whipped cream, shaping it into a smooth dome
  18. Chill at least 2 hours to set

    19. Cover with Marzipan
  19. Roll out green marzipan on powdered-sugar-dusted surface
  20. Drape over the cake and smooth gently
  21. Trim excess around the base

Gravlax

Norway

Ingredients

    For Salmon
  • Salmon Filets
  • Salt
  • White Sugar
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Fresh Dill(chopped)

    • For Gravlax Sauce

  • Egg Yolks
  • Salt
  • Vegetables Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • White Sugar
  • White Wine Vinegar
  • Mustard
  • Ground White Pepper
  • Fresh Dill(Finely Chopped)

Methods

    Salmon
  1. Trim the salmon fillets;Scrape the skin;rmove all bones
  2. In a small bowl, mix salt,sugar and pepper
  3. Sprinkle half of the salt mixture and half of the dill in the bottom of a glass pan and
  4. Place the salmon fillet skin-side-down in the pan
  5. Sprinkle remaining salt mixture and dill on the top and press it lightly into the salmon
  6. Cover the pan with plastic wrap and place it in the refrigerator for 3-4 days. turn the salmon every day
  7. On the 3rd or 4th day, rinse the salmon under cold water
  8. Move to a cutting board and use a sharp knife to cut very thin slices

    9. Gravlax Sauce
  9. In a medium bowl, stir the egg yolks with the salt until thick.
  10. Slowly pour in the oil wile whisking quickly it has a consistency similar to mayo
  11. Stir the sugar,vinegar,mustard,and pepper into sauce; Add dill before serving.

Lefse

Norway

Ingredients

  • A lot of potatoes(peeled)
  • Salt
  • White Sugar
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • All-Purpose Flour

Methods

  1. Place potatoes in a large pot and cover with water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender; drain
  2. Run hot potatoes through a potato ricer into a large bowl
  3. Beat butter, cream, salt, and sugar into riced potatoes; Let cool to room temperature
  4. Stir flour into potato mixture to form a soft dough; Pull off pieces of dough and form into walnut-sized balls
  5. Lightly flour a clean cloth and roll out lefse balls to 1/8-inch thick
  6. Heat a griddle over high heat
  7. On the 3rd or 4th day, rinse the salmon under cold water
  8. Cook lefse on the hot griddle until brown blisters form

  3. Season with salt and pepper; let cool

  5. Stir in mashed potatoes, salt, and white pepper
  6. Remove from heat

  7. Mix sour cream and eggs until smooth

  17. Remove with a slotted spoon

Sernik

Poland

Ingredients

  • Unsalted Butter
  • Farmers Cheese
  • White Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Pudding
  • Heavy Cream
  • Potato Starch

Methods

  1. Preheat oven to 340°F
  2. Prepare a 10-inch springform pan by greasing it with butter and lining the bottom with parchment paper, or dusting it with breadcrumbs
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened butter with sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Incorporate egg yolks one at a time, ensuring each is fully blended before adding the next
  5. Gradually mix in the ground farmers' cheese
  6. Add the vanilla pudding mix and potato starch, blending until smooth.
  7. Slowly pour in the heavy cream, continuing to mix until well combined
  8. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites to stiff peaks.
  9. Gently fold whipped whites into the cheesecake batter. Do not overmix
  10. Pour the cheesecake into the prepared pan, Smooth the top with a spatula .
  11. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheesecake is set and the top is golden brown.
  12. Turn off the oven, slightly crack open the door, and allow the cheesecake to cool inside for an hour.
  13. Afterward, remove from the oven and let it cool completely before removing from the pan

Tavë Kosi

Albania

Ingredients

    For Lamb and Rice Base
  • Butter
  • Lamb(cut into medium chunks)
  • Onion
  • White Rice
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Water or lamb broth

    • For Yogurt Sauce
  • Plain full-fat yogurt
  • Large eggs
  • All-purpose flour
  • Butter
  • Ground Nutmeg(optional)
  • Salt to taste

Methods

    Prepare the Lamb
  1. In a large skillet or saucepan, melt butter over medium heat
  2. Add lamb chunks to the oil and sear until brown on all sides
  3. Add the chopped onion, cook until softened
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Add your choice of liquid, cover, and simmer until the lamb is tender

    6. Cook the Rice
  6. Rinse rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  7. Add the rice to the lamb pot and cook, uncovered, until the rice is half-cooked
  8. Remove from heat and set aside

    9. Make the yogurt Sauce
  9. In a mixing bowl, whisk eggs thoroughly
  10. Add the flour and whisk until smooth
  11. Gradually add yogurt and salt, mix until it becomes lump-free and combined fully
  12. In a small saucepan, melt butter and add yogurt mixture
  13. Add nutmeg if using

    14. Assemble and Bake
  14. Preheat your oven to 350°F
  15. Take a deep baking dish and grease it with a little butter or oil
  16. Transfer lamb and rice mixture into dish
  17. Pour yogurt mixture over lamb and rice
  18. Bake for about 45 minutes

Flija

Albania

Ingredients

  • Unsalted Butter(melted)
  • Whole milk(warmed)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Plain whole-milk yogurt
  • Baking soda
  • Neutral oil(grapeseed)

Methods

    Prepare the base Batter
  1. Sift flour into a bowl
  2. Whisk eggs, then slowly add warmed milk
  3. Combine wet mixture with flour, salt, and baking soda
  4. Whisk until the consistency of heavy cream
  5. Cover and rest batter for 20 minutes

    6. Preheat and Prepare the Skillet
  6. Preheat oven to 375°F with a cast-iron skillet inside for 30 minutes
  7. Remove and brush with neutral oil

    8. Build the First Layer
  8. Pour1/2 cup of batter into a hot skillet and tilt to spread thinly
  9. Spoon 3 tbsp yogurt over the surface, leave a small border
  10. Bake for 8 minutes
  11. Remove the skillet and brush the top with melted butter
  12. Add another 1/2 cup batter, then 3 tbsp yogurt
  13. Bake for 7 minutes
  14. Repeat for 6 total layers, reducing bake time by 1 minute each round
  15. After the last layer, bake until golden brown and fully set
  16. Let rest for 10 minutes

Pizza Margheritas

Italy

Ingredients

    For the Dough
  • Water
  • Pizza flour
  • Salt
  • Dry yeast

    • For the Sauce and Toppings
  • Pureed, crushed canned San Marzano tomatoes
  • Kosher salt to taste
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Methods

    Night Before Baking
  1. In a large bowl, dissolve the salt in the water
  2. Add some of the flour and mix until it forms a crepe-like batter
  3. Add yeast and mix well with your fingers until dissolved
  4. Gradually add the remaining flour
  5. When the dough comes together, transfer to a clean surface and knead for 10 minutes
  6. Cover and rest the dough for 10 minutes
  7. Knead again for another 10 minutes, until smooth and elastic. Let rest for 10 minutes to let the gluten relax
  8. Form the dough into a big smooth ball. Place it in a lightly floured bowl, cover with plastic wrap or a damp cloth, and let it rest overnight on the kitchen counter

    9. Shaping dough balls and cold fermentation
  9. After 8-12 hours, divide the dough into 8 equal pieces
  10. Form each piece into smooth dough balls
  11. Place the dough balls on a lightly floured baking sheet, cover tightly with plastic wrap, or place in airtight dough containers.
  12. Refrigerate for 6–48 hours.

    13. Day of Baking
  13. Take the dough balls out of the fridge 3 hours before baking. Let rest at room temperature, covered.
  14. Lightly flour work surface
  15. Gently flatten dough, leaving a thicker outer rim
  16. Carefully stretch the dough into a 10–12-inch circle
  17. Preheat your oven to its highest setting (usually 550°F), with a pizza stone placed on the middle rack. Let the stone heat for at least 30 minutes.
  18. Spoon crushed San Marzano tomato sauce over the stretched dough.
  19. Spread a few slices of the fresh mozzarella over the top of the sauce
  20. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the top in a spiral motion. Add a couple leaves of basil
  21. In a home oven at 550°F: about 10–15 minutes.

Tiramisu

Italy

Ingredients

  • Savoiardi ladyfingers
  • Cold mascarpone cheese
  • Medium eggs, separated
  • Sugar
  • Strong espresso coffee
  • Marsala wine
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder

Methods

  1. Prepare the coffee; let it cool completely
  2. Add Marsala wine if desired
  3. Separate the egg whites from the yolks. Place them in two different bowls.
  4. Whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  5. In a separate bowl, beat the egg yolks with the sugar until the mixture becomes pale, smooth and slightly thickened
  6. Add the mascarpone to the whipped yolks. Gently mix until smooth and fully incorporated
  7. Fold the whipped egg whites into the mascarpone mixture. Use a spatula or wooden spoon and mix gently from the bottom up, so you do not deflate the cream. Continue until smooth and airy.
  8. Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the cooled coffee for 1 to 2 seconds
  9. Arrange the soaked ladyfingers in a single layer in your serving dish
  10. Spread half of the mascarpone cream evenly over the ladyfingers.
  11. Add a second layer of dipped ladyfingers, then cover with the remaining mascarpone cream. Smooth the surface
  12. Dust generously with unsweetened cocoa powder.
  13. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours before serving.

Moussaka with Eggplant

Greece

Ingredients

    For the Eggplant
  • Globe Eggplants
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Breadcrumbs

    • For the Meat Sauce

  • large yellow onion, finely chopped
  • ground lamb or beef
  • Dried oregano
  • ground cinnamon
  • ground nutmeg
  • Paprika
  • Red wine
  • Can diced tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Beef broth(warmed)

    • For the bechamel

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 2% milk(warmed)
  • Ground Nutmeg
  • Large eggs

Methods

    Prep the eggplant
  1. Trim ends and slice eggplants into ¼‑inch slabs
  2. Lay slices in one layer, sprinkle with salt, and let sit 30 minutes to draw out bitterness.
  3. Pat dry and set aside.

    4. Make the meat sauce
  4. Heat olive oil in a skillet; sauté chopped onion until golden.
  5. Add ground lamb or beef; cook until fully browned
  6. Drain excess fat and return to heat.
  7. Stir in oregano, cinnamon, pepper, nutmeg, paprika, and salt
  8. Add red wine; boil 1 minute to reduce
  9. Stir in diced tomatoes, sugar, and hot beef broth.
  10. Simmer 20–30 minutes until thick and fragrant.

    11. Boil the Eggplant
  11. Turn broiler to high.
  12. Brush a sheet pan with olive oil
  13. Arrange eggplant slices in one layer; brush both sides with olive oil.
  14. Broil about 6 inches from heat, flipping once, until softened and golden.
  15. Set aside

    16. Prepare to Bake
  16. Turn off broiler; preheat oven to 350°F.
  17. Lightly oil a 9×13‑inch baking dish.

    18. Make the Béchamel
  18. Heat olive oil in a saucepan; stir in flour, salt, and pepper
  19. Cook until golden, stirring continuously
  20. Slowly whisk in warmed milk
  21. Cook 5–7 minutes until thick enough to coat a spoon
  22. Season with nutmeg
  23. Temper eggs with a ladle of hot béchamel, then whisk back into the pot
  24. Cook 2 more minutes until smooth and creamy.

    25. Assemble the Moussaka
  25. Layer half the eggplant slices in the baking dish
  26. Spread all the meat sauce over them
  27. Add remaining eggplant slices on top.
  28. Pour béchamel over the entire surface and spread evenly.
  29. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs.
  30. Bake on center rake for 45 minutes
  31. Let sit for 10 minutes to set

Ekmek Kataifi

Greece

Ingredients

    For the Base
  • Kataifi Dough
  • Butter(melted)
  • Pistachios(chopped)

    • For the Syrup

  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Lemon peel
  • Cinnamon Stick

    • For the Custard

  • Whole milk(cold)
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • CornStarch
  • Egg yolks
  • Butter
  • Powdered mastic (optional)

    • For the Whipped Cream

  • Heavy whipping cream (cold)
  • Icing Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract

Methods

    Prep the Kataifi Base
  1. Preheat oven to 340°F
  2. Unroll kataifi dough and gently separate the strands with your fingers
  3. Butter the bottom of a square baking dish.
  4. Spread kataifi evenly in the dish.
  5. Pour melted butter over the dough
  6. Bake 30–45 minutes until crispy and golden.
  7. Remove from oven and let cool completely.

    8. Make the Custard
  8. Whisk egg yolks with half the sugar until fluffy.
  9. Add a few tablespoons of milk + cornstarch; whisk until smooth.
  10. Heat remaining milk with sugar and vanilla until it just boils
  11. Slowly ladle ⅓ of the hot milk into the egg mixture while whisking.
  12. Return everything to the pot over medium heat.
  13. Whisk continuously until thick, smooth, and creamy
  14. Remove from heat; stir in butter
  15. Spread custard in a tray, cover with plastic wrap touching the surface, and cool.

    16. Prepare the Syrup
  16. Add sugar, water, lemon peel, and cinnamon stick to a pot.
  17. Bring to a boil; simmer 3 minutes without stirring.
  18. Remove from heat and let cool until lukewarm

    19. Syrup the base
  19. Ensure kataifi base is cold.
  20. Slowly ladle lukewarm syrup over it, allowing each ladle to absorb fully.
  21. Let sit to cool and soak evenly

    22. Make the Whipped cream
  22. Add cold heavy cream, icing sugar, and vanilla to a chilled bowl
  23. Whip on high speed until medium peaks form (about 1 minute)

    24. Assemble the Dessert
  24. Spread cooled custard evenly over the syrup‑soaked kataifi base
  25. Top with whipped cream and smooth the surface
  26. Sprinkle with chopped pistachios and a pinch of cinnamon.
  27. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.

Quiche Lorraine

France

Ingredients

    For the Crust
  • Foolproof Shortcrust Pastry

    • For the Quiche

  • large yellow onions
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt,
  • thick-cut bacon
  • Whole milk(cold)
  • heavy cream
  • Ground Black pepper
  • Grated Nutmeg
  • Eggs
  • grated Comté, Gruyère, or Emmenthal cheese

Methods

    Prep the Crust
  1. Make two batches of shortcrust pastry
  2. Combine both batches, form into a ball, and roll into a 16‑inch circle (¼‑inch thick).
  3. Fit dough into a 9‑inch springform pan and prick the bottom with a fork.
  4. Freeze the pastry‑lined pan for 30 minutes.

    5. Blind Bake the Crust
  5. Leave pastry overhanging the pan.
  6. Bake straight from freezer for 45 minutes until dry and golden.

    7. Cook the onions and bacon
  7. Heat olive oil in a large pan.
  8. Add sliced onions + 1 tsp salt..
  9. Cover and cook 15 minutes.
  10. Uncover, reduce heat, and cook 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes, until caramelized.
  11. Transfer to a bowl to cool.
  12. Cook bacon in a skillet until crisp but not dry
  13. Transfer to the bowl with onions and let cool

    14. Make the custard
  14. In a large bowl, mix milk, cream, eggs, remaining salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  15. Beat with a hand mixer until very frothy

    16. Assemble the Quiche
  16. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  17. Place springform pan on a baking sheet.Spread half the onion‑bacon mixture over the crust.
  18. Pour half the custard over it
  19. Sprinkle half the cheese.
  20. Add remaining onion‑bacon mixture.
  21. Re‑froth remaining custard and pour it in.
  22. Top with remaining cheese.
  23. Bake for 90 minutes, until the top is golden and the center is just set but still slightly jiggly.
  24. Cool on a rack for 2 hours.Refrigerate for at least 3 hours, ideally overnight, to fully set

Ratatouille

France

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Zucchini
  • Yellow squash
  • Eggplant
  • Red bell pepper
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Peeled Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Thyme
  • Fresh Basil
  • Extra Virgin olive oil
  • Herbes de Provence
  • Kosher Salt

Methods

  1. Heat olive oil until shimmering. Add diced onion and cook until deeply golden and caramelized
  2. In a separate pan, sauté zucchini and squash until lightly browned. Season with salt and Herbes de Provence.
  3. Sauté eggplant until browned and some moisture evaporates..
  4. In the onion pan, add diced bell peppers and cook until softened.
  5. Add eggplant to the onion-pepper mixture.
  6. Add garlic, thyme, basil, crushed tomatoes, and cherry tomatoes.
  7. Stir in the pre‑cooked zucchini and squash. Mix well.
  8. Cook on medium‑low for 45–60 minutes, uncovered or loosely covered, stirring occasionally.

Moules Frites

Belgium

Ingredients

  • Unsalted Butter
  • Large Shallots
  • Garlic
  • White wine
  • Kosher Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Fresh mussels
  • Grated lemon zest
  • Fresh Basil
  • Finely chopped fresh flat-leaf parsly
  • Frozen fries
  • Mayonnaise

Methods

  1. Melt butter in a 6–8 quart Dutch oven over medium‑low heat.
  2. Add finely chopped shallots and sliced garlic.
  3. Cook 8–10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until very soft but not browned.
  4. Increase heat to high.
  5. Add white wine, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  6. Bring to a boil and cook about 4 minutes, reducing the liquid by half..
  7. Add cleaned mussels to the pot.
  8. Cover and cook over high heat.Shake/toss the pot every minute to help mussels settle and open
  9. Steam 5–6 minutes, until all mussels have opened.
  10. Remove from heat.
  11. Stir in lemon zest, lemon juice, and chopped parsley.
  12. Serve mussels immediately with French fries (fresh or frozen) and mayonnaise.

Vol-au-Vent

Belgium

Ingredients

    For the Puff Pastry
  • flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Egg

    • For the Sauce Financière

  • Lean ham
  • Mignonette
  • Thyme
  • Bay leaf
  • Mushroom and Truffle trimmings
  • Consommé
  • Dry Madeira

Methods

    Prep the Puff Pastry
  1. Sift flour onto a board and make a well in the center.
  2. Add salt and water; mix and knead until smooth and elastic
  3. Form into a ball and rest 25 minutes..
  4. Roll dough into a 20×20 cm square.
  5. Place kneaded butter in the center; fold dough over to enclose it
  6. Rest 10 minutes in a cool place.
  7. Roll dough into a 60×20 cm rectangle, 1.5 cm thick.
  8. Fold into thirds
  9. Roll again in the opposite direction
  10. Repeat 4 more turns, resting 10 minutes between each.
  11. After 6 total turns, the puff pastry is ready
  12. Roll pastry to 5 mm thickness
  13. Cut a 15 cm circle for the base; place on a dampened baking sheet.
  14. Cut a second circle, then remove the center to form a ring.
  15. Place ring on top of the base and press lightly.
  16. Rest 10 minutes
  17. Brush top with beaten egg
  18. Cut a small circle in the center to use as the lid
  19. Bake at 350°F for 20–25 minutes until risen and golden
  20. Remove lid and let pastry cool.

    21. Make the Sauce Financière
  21. Combine shredded ham, mignonette, thyme, bay leaf, mushroom & truffle trimmings, and 2 glasses Madeira in a saucepan.
  22. Reduce over low heat.
  23. Add consommé; reduce by half.
  24. Strain and return to heat.
  25. Add ½ glass Madeira and reduce in a bain‑marie until thickened.

    26. Assemble
  26. Fill the baked vol‑au‑vent with warm sauce financière.
  27. Place the pastry lid on top.
  28. Serve immediately.

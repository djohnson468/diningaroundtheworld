China

These are some popular traditional dishes from China, including foods often made for Lunar New Year, the Lantern Festival, and other celebratory family meals. There are also a few beginner friendly dishes at the end you can try to make.

01

Jiaozi

Dumplings. Traditionally Made For Lunar New Year.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Water
  • Ground meat (pork, beef, or chicken)
  • Cabbage or Chinese chives
  • Scallions
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Equipement

  1. Mixing bowl
  2. Rolling pin
  3. Knife
  4. Cutting board
  5. Pot (for boiling)
  6. Pan or steamer

About

This savory dish is soft, comforting, and often shared with family to welcome the new year together.

About

These rolls are crispy and savory, making them a festive favorite that adds crunch and warmth to celebration meals.
